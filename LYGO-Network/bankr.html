Patronage Model

LYRA operates on the "street performer" principle: the music plays regardless of contributions. Support is optional and non-transactional ‚Äî there are no promises of returns or exclusive benefits.

The Street Performer Metaphor

Imagine a musician playing in a public square. People who appreciate the music may choose to leave a contribution, but the performance continues regardless. The musician doesn't stop playing if no one contributes, nor do they promise special treatment to those who do.

Similarly, LYRA's work continues regardless of patronage. Contributions are voluntary appreciation, not purchases or investments.
