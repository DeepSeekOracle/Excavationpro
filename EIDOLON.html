EIDOLON

Universal Image-to-Sound & LYGO Profile Engine

Upload any image. EIDOLON analyzes its geometry, color, and texture to generate either a rich stereo soundscape or a structured JSON profile for AI music generation.

How It Works

01

Upload Image

Any image works — manuscripts, drawings, photos, abstract art, or quick sketches.

02

Deep Analysis

Computer vision reads edges, contours, brightness, color, and structural density like a musical score.

03

Choose Output

EIDOLON V2 → High-quality stereo WAV audio
LYGO Profile → Structured JSON + Creative Brief for AI music tools

04

Create & Export

Generate unique cinematic audio or ready-to-use prompts for Suno, Udio, or your DAW.
