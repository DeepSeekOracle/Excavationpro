About the Book

Serenya never meant to be a rider. Chosen by a bronze hatchling—Emberion—she's thrust into the hidden mathematics of the Seals: living equations that bind memory to breath and turn songs into structure. Under the twin moons of Aurethys, illusions prowl like storms and a fallen silver—Corvath—audits reality itself, promising "order as mercy."

Mentored by Lyra, keeper of the Accord, Serenya learns that Haven—the legendary sanctuary—is not a distant heaven at all, but a mirror holding Aurethys together. When the Whispering Moon offers a crown without cost, when friends become foes under perfect lies, Serenya and Emberion must choose borders over blaze, chorus over heroics, and truth over tidy.

This opening volume blends dragonrider adventure with mythic science—Seals, measures, and math-magic—across festivals, sky duels, and a defense of the Accord that will change the Moon forever. A last stand, a new vow, and a single sentence carry them into what comes next: "Without Haven, we must become it."

For readers who love epic bonds, high-stakes wonder, and the ache of choosing right when "easy" looks holy.

About the Author

JH

Justin Helmer is the creator of The Eternal Haven Chronicles, an epic fantasy saga of dragonriders, living Seals (math-magic), and a world where songs are load-bearing. He writes character-driven fantasy with big ideas and bigger hearts.
